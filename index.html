Who I Am

Beyond the title.

I'm Mohamad Nihad Alsufe — a CEO, Full-Stack Developer, and Creator based in the UAE. Started writing production code early on. Never stopped.

Right now I lead Organic Vision (ovm.ae) as both CEO and CTO — an AI media and marketing agency building intelligent solutions for brands across the region. I own the entire stack: business strategy, product architecture, and client delivery.

On the engineering side I architect cross-platform production apps in Flutter and Firebase, build backend infrastructure with cloud functions and real-time systems, and design full-stack web products in Next.js and React. My project NearSchool is a live Flutter/Firebase app tackling school-parent communication at scale.

Beyond code, I founded the Syrian Speedcubing Community, am nationally ranked #4 in Syria as a competitive cuber, run the NS-Cubing channel, and represent Cubuzzle as their Middle East brand partner. GCAA-licensed drone operator and a recurring guest on Dubai Sports TV.
